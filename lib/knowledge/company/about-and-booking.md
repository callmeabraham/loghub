## About us

**LogHub Spedition GmbH** is a mid-size moving and logistics company headquartered in Hamburg, with a satellite depot in Berlin. We handle household moves, office relocations, and furniture/freight transport across Germany and neighboring EU countries. We employ 34 people across dispatch, packing crews, and driving staff, and run a fleet of 11 vehicles (from 7.5t transporters up to 40t trucks for international jobs).

## Service area

- **Domestic (Germany)**: all federal states, next-day availability in most cases within 150km of Hamburg or Berlin.
- **International (EU)**: regular corridors to the Netherlands, Belgium, France, Austria, and Switzerland; other EU destinations on request with 5-7 working days' lead time.
- We do not currently serve moves outside the EU/EEA/Switzerland — for those, we can refer customers to a partner freight forwarder.

## How booking works

1. **Initial request**: customer describes the move (origin, destination, approximate date, rough size — e.g. "3-room apartment").
2. **Qualification**: we ask for the missing essentials — exact addresses (including floor/elevator availability), preferred date and flexibility, estimated volume or room count, any special items (piano, safe, artwork, aquarium, oversized furniture), and insurance needs.
3. **Site assessment**: for moves above roughly 40m³ or with challenging access (no elevator above 3rd floor, narrow streets), we do a free video or in-person walkthrough before quoting.
4. **Quote**: sent within 1-2 business days after the initial request, valid for 30 days.
5. **Confirmation**: a signed moving contract (Umzugsvertrag) and a deposit (typically 20%) confirm the booking; balance is due on completion.
