## Standard liability (default, included in every move)

Every move is automatically covered by the statutory AMÖ-standard liability: up to **EUR 620 per cubic metre** of moving volume for loss or damage during loading, transport, or unloading (see the AMÖ terms and HGB Frachtvertrag regulatory sources for the legal basis). This is included at no extra cost — no action needed from the customer.

## Additional (Transport-)Versicherung — recommended for higher-value moves

For moves where total goods value exceeds what the EUR 620/m³ default would cover, we offer additional transport insurance:

- **Extended coverage**: insures goods at declared value rather than the per-m³ cap, for a premium of roughly 1-1.5% of declared value (minimum premium €35)
- Must be arranged **before** the move — it cannot be added retroactively after loading has started
- Requires a rough inventory/valuation of higher-value items (electronics, art, antiques, jewelry left in furniture — note: valuables should generally be transported by the customer personally, not packed)

## Piano transport

Pianos (upright and grand) are a specialty item requiring:
- **Advance notice** — at least 5 business days before the move, so we can schedule a crew trained in piano handling and the right equipment (piano board/skid, ramps, straps)
- **Specialty coverage**: pianos are excluded from the standard EUR 620/m³ cap by default (their per-item value routinely exceeds it) — piano moves are quoted with **declared-value coverage** included in the piano transport fee, not the general per-m³ default
- **Access check required**: stairs, doorways, and elevator dimensions must be confirmed in advance; grand pianos sometimes require a window/balcony hoist for narrow stairwells, quoted separately
- Typical piano transport fee: **€250-€450** for an upright piano, **€450-€900** for a grand piano, depending on distance and access difficulty — always confirmed with a specific quote after the access check

## Other special items

- **Safes**: weight and access must be confirmed in advance; heavy safes (>150kg) may require special equipment and a surcharge
- **Aquariums**: must be fully drained and prepared by the customer before the crew arrives (we do not transport livestock/fish — customer must arrange separate transport for fish)
- **Artwork/antiques**: eligible for declared-value coverage same as other high-value items; custom crating available on request

## Claims process

Report any visible damage to the crew lead **before they leave** and note it on the delivery confirmation — this mirrors the CMR/HGB requirement to note reservations at the time of delivery. Claims for concealed damage must be reported in writing within **7 days** of delivery. We aim to resolve claims within 14 business days; unresolved consumer disputes can be brought to the AMÖ mediation office free of charge.
