## How we price a move

Pricing is based on four factors: **volume** (cubic metres of goods), **distance**, **access complexity** (floor, elevator, parking distance from the truck to the door), and **service level** (self-pack vs. full-service pack).

## Indicative rates (domestic, Germany)

These are illustrative starting rates for a typical Hamburg-area move; final quotes always follow a volume/access assessment:

| Apartment size | Approx. volume | Local move (< 50km) | Long-distance (> 300km) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1-room | 10-15 m³ | from €490 | from €890 |
| 2-room | 20-28 m³ | from €790 | from €1,390 |
| 3-room | 30-42 m³ | from €1,190 | from €2,090 |
| 4+ room / house | 45m³+ | custom quote | custom quote |

## What's included in the base price

- Loading, transport, unloading by a trained crew
- Basic furniture disassembly/reassembly of standard items (beds, wardrobes, shelving)
- Furniture blankets and basic protective wrapping
- Standard liability coverage per the AMÖ terms (see the insurance & liability policy)

## What costs extra

- **Full packing service** (crew packs boxes, not just furniture): from €6-9 per box-equivalent, or a flat packing-day rate on request
- **Piano and specialty-item transport**: quoted separately — see the insurance & special items policy
- **Additional insurance coverage** above the statutory default
- **Difficult access surcharge**: no elevator above 3rd floor, or parking further than 20m from the entrance, or narrow streets requiring a smaller shuttle vehicle
- **Storage** (Möbellager): from €25/m³ per month for interim storage between moves
- **Weekend surcharge**: see the availability & scheduling policy

## Payment terms

20% deposit on contract signature, balance due on the day of the move (bank transfer or card). Business customers can request invoicing with 14-day payment terms, subject to approval.
