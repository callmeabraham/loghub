## Cancellation policy

- **More than 14 days before the move date**: full refund of deposit, no fee.
- **7-14 days before**: 50% of deposit retained as a cancellation fee.
- **Less than 7 days before**: full deposit retained; if the crew and vehicle were already dispatched or en route, up to 25% of the total quoted price may additionally be charged.
- **Rescheduling** (rather than cancelling) is free of charge if requested more than 5 days before the original date, subject to availability on the new date.
- Cancellations due to documented emergencies (medical, etc.) are assessed case by case and may waive fees at management discretion.

## Parking permits (Halteverbotszone)

For moves in dense urban areas (especially Hamburg and Berlin city centers), we strongly recommend arranging a **temporary no-parking zone (Halteverbotszone)** in front of the property for the move date, to guarantee the truck can park close to the entrance:

- We can arrange this on the customer's behalf for a service fee of **€80-€150** depending on the city/district, provided we're notified **at least 10 business days** in advance (many city authorities require this lead time for the permit and signage).
- Alternatively, the customer can apply for the permit themselves directly with the local Ordnungsamt/district office — we'll provide the required truck dimensions and move date for the application.
- Without a secured parking zone, longer carrying distances from the truck to the entrance may trigger the difficult-access surcharge described in the pricing policy, and in the worst case (no legal parking available at all) can delay or reschedule the move.

## Weekend & availability

- **Weekday moves**: standard pricing, best availability.
- **Saturday moves**: available, **15% surcharge** on the base price, subject to crew availability — recommend booking at least 3 weeks ahead in peak season (May-September, and around month-end/quarter-end).
- **Sunday & public holiday moves**: only available for commercial/office relocations by special arrangement, **25% surcharge**, minimum 2 weeks' notice.
- Peak season (May-September) and month-end dates book out fastest; we recommend requesting a quote at least 3-4 weeks in advance during these periods, and 1-2 weeks otherwise.

## Crew sizing

Crew size is set based on volume and access complexity, not negotiable per customer preference (it's a safety and speed matter):

| Volume | Typical crew | Typical duration (local move) |
|---|---|---|
| up to 15 m³ | 2 people | half day |
| 15-30 m³ | 3 people | half to full day |
| 30-45 m³ | 3-4 people | full day |
| 45m³+ / house | 4-6 people | full day, sometimes split over 2 days |

Piano transport or difficult access (stairs-only, no elevator, 4th floor+) typically adds one extra crew member regardless of overall volume.
