## Core services

- **Household moves**: full-load and part-load, local and long-distance
- **Office & commercial relocations**: including IT equipment handling and after-hours/weekend scheduling to minimize business downtime
- **Möbeldemontage & -montage (furniture disassembly & reassembly)**: standard for beds, wardrobes (Schrankwand), shelving units, and modular furniture; included in the base price for standard items, quoted separately for complex systems (e.g. built-in kitchens, wall-mounted units)
- **Packing service**: full-service packing (crew brings materials and packs everything) or partial packing (fragile/valuable items only, customer packs the rest)
- **Storage (Möbellager)**: short- and long-term climate-controlled storage between move-out and move-in dates
- **Specialty item transport**: pianos, safes, artwork, large aquariums, antiques — see the insurance & special items policy for specifics
- **Kitchen disassembly/reassembly (Küchenmontage)**: available as an add-on for built-in kitchens; requires a site visit to quote accurately, as connection points (water, gas, electric) vary
- **Disposal/decluttering (Entrümpelung)**: optional add-on for customers who want unwanted furniture removed and disposed of or donated during the move

## Furniture disassembly (Möbeldemontage) — what's included

Standard disassembly/reassembly covers: bed frames, standard wardrobes, shelving, dining tables, and modular furniture systems using standard fittings. **Not included** in the base price: disconnecting/reconnecting gas or electrical appliances, wall-mounted TVs or shelving requiring new drilling at the destination, and built-in kitchen units — these require a specialist add-on and, per our liability terms, are only covered under the AMÖ standard freight liability cap if explicitly included in the written quote.

## Items that trigger a surcharge beyond the base move

Pianos, safes over 150kg, aquariums, built-in kitchens, and any wall-mounted item requiring new drilling at the destination are **not** included in the base per-m³ price — each is quoted individually (see the insurance & special items policy for piano and safe pricing specifics, and the table above for kitchens/wall-mounts).

## International moves

For EU destinations, we handle door-to-door moves with our own fleet on regular corridors (NL, BE, FR, AT, CH) and via trusted partner carriers for other EU countries. See the international moves policy for lead times, customs notes, and what's different from a domestic move.

## What we don't do

- We are not licensed for ADR (dangerous goods) transport of quantities above the small-quantity exemption thresholds — customers must declare hazardous items (gas cylinders, large chemical quantities, ammunition) in advance so we can assess whether we can take them or need to refer to a specialist carrier.
- We do not provide long-haul freight/logistics for commercial cargo unrelated to a move or relocation.
